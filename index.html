Who We Are

Premium Agriculture

TazVerde LTD is a UK-headquartered company operating across Uganda, Nigeria, Ghana, and Tanzania, focused on the global supply of premium-quality agricultural products. Our specialty lies in vanilla, cocoa, coffee, spices, and more — all meticulously sourced to preserve natural purity and flavor.

We collaborate with established, eco-conscious farm networks that uphold ethical harvesting and maintain full traceability from farm to export.
